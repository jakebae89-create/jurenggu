## App name (max 30 chars)

## Short description (max 80 chars)

## Full description (max 4000 chars)
```
Enjoy a game the people of ancient Silla played a thousand years ago — right in your hand.

“Juryeonggu: Silla Dice” brings to life the Juryeonggu (酒令具), a 14-sided wooden die unearthed from the Donggung Palace and Wolji Pond (Anapji) in Gyeongju, the ancient Silla capital. With 6 square faces and 8 hexagonal faces — 14 in total — each side is carved with a playful forfeit (penalty) to act out.

■ How to play
1. Register the players (up to 14).
2. On your turn, tap the “Roll” button.
3. The 3D die tumbles, and you perform the forfeit shown on the face it lands on.
4. Pass the turn to the next player.

■ Key features
• A 3D 14-sided die modeled on the proportions of the real artifact — spin, zoom in and out with your finger.
• 14 original Silla-era forfeits included (Chinese characters + Korean reading + explanation).
• Freely edit the forfeits — make up your own house rules for your group.
• Player registration and automatic turn rotation.
• Lightweight app that runs offline, no internet required.
• Settings and players are stored only on your device, for peace of mind.

■ Examples of traditional forfeits
• Geumseong-jangmu (禁聲作舞) — Dance without making a sound.
• Eumjin-daeso (飮盡大笑) — Drink it all, then laugh out loud.
• Gongyeong-sigwa (空詠詩過) — Recite a poem.
• Jachang-goeraeman (自唱怪來晚) — Sing the “Goeraeman” song.

Perfect for breaking the ice and livening up gatherings with friends, colleagues, and family. Experience the wit and spirit of the people of Silla at your own table today!

※ This app is themed on a traditional Korean drinking culture and contains references to alcohol. Please drink responsibly and only if you are of legal drinking age.
```
